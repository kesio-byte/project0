Who Are We

We are in Chikonde, 10kms from Zalewa turnoff and 75kms from Neno turnoff, thus between Chikonde markert,Chiwale and Neno girls Secondry School along Litchowa and Neno turnoff road, thus in the South rigion of Malawi.

Chikonde is the main town of Neno Boma.It is the most populous town in Neno district,with a metropolitan area of over 350 thousand, inhabitats. Standing between the rivers Chiwale, Asumi and Wankhulumadzi, Chikonde has been a mojor settlement for two millennia,its history going back to its founding by the Ngoni people, who migritated from SouthAfrica 19 centruary ago and name it Chikonde because of the red soil type.
